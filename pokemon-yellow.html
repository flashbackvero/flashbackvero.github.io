Only six sealed copies of the Wal-Mart Movie Ticket Sticker Pokemon Yellow are known to exist, and just one carries a WATA 9.8 A++ grade. This is that copy: a true population-one grail.

This is a factory-sealed copy of Pokemon Yellow Version: Special Pikachu Edition in the elusive Wal-Mart Movie Ticket Sticker packaging variation, an early retailer promotion connected to the era of Pokemon: The First Movie.

Graded WATA 9.8 A++, this copy combines a near-perfect box grade with WATA's highest-tier seal designation. More importantly, PSA Games' current population data records only six sealed examples of this exact Wal-Mart Movie Ticket Sticker variant. Of those, just one is graded 9.8 A++.

That makes this copy a true population one: the sole highest-graded certified example currently recorded for one of the rarest retail variants of Pokemon Yellow.

Population reports can change as new copies are discovered and submitted, but as of the current report, this is not simply a high-grade Pokemon game. It is the top certified example of a historic 1999 Pokemon promotion.
