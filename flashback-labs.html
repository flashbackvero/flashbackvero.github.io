Mobile Game Support

Having trouble with one of our mobile games, found a bug, or want to suggest a feature? Email flashbackvero@gmail.com and we'll get back to you as soon as we can — usually within a few days.

To help us fix things fast, include: the game name, your device model, and a quick description of what happened (screenshots welcome).
