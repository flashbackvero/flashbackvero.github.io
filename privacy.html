Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

This privacy policy covers the Vero Flashback website (flashbackvero.com) and the apps we publish, including Particle Simulator — Physics Sandbox.

Nothing. Our games and website do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no analytics trackers, no ads, and no third-party data collection in our apps.

Some of our games save your progress and settings (for example, a saved chess game or simulator preferences) using your browser or device's local storage. This data never leaves your device, and you can erase it at any time by clearing the app or browser data.

Children's privacy

Because we collect no data at all, our apps do not collect any information from children.
